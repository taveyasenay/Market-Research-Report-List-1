<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyolefins (TPO) are categorized into three main types based on their properties and applications. H-Type TPOs are characterized by high heat resistance and are often used in automotive and industrial applications. L-Type TPOs are designed for lower heat resistance, providing flexibility and ease of processing, commonly utilized in consumer goods and soft-touch applications. P-Type TPOs focus on enhanced performance, including superior impact resistance and durability, making them suitable for demanding environments like construction and packaging.</p></p>
