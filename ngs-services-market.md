<p><p>NGS (Next Generation Sequencing) services encompass various market types, notably RNA-Seq and exome sequencing. RNA-Seq involves analyzing the transcriptome to assess gene expression and identify novel transcripts and splice variants, crucial for understanding cellular functions and disease mechanisms. In contrast, exome sequencing focuses on the protein-coding regions of the genome, which represent a small but significant portion associated with hereditary disorders. Both services are pivotal in genomics, personalized medicine, and ongoing research into complex diseases.</p></p>
