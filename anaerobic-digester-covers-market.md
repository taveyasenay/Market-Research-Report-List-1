<p><p>Anaerobic digester covers are essential for capturing biogas produced during organic matter decomposition. Fixed covers are rigid structures installed over digesters, providing durability and protection. Buoyant covers float on the liquid surface, adapting to volume changes while maintaining a seal. Gasholder covers act like a balloon, allowing for gas expansion and contraction. Hydroseal covers utilize a water moat for a tight seal, preventing gas escape. Other types may include innovative designs tailored for specific operational needs or environments.</p></p>
