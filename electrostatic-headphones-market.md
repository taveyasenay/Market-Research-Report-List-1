<p><strong>The Electrostatic Headphones Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrostatic headphones are categorized into active and passive market types based on their operational mechanisms. Active electrostatic headphones utilize an external power source to energize the diaphragm, enhancing sound quality and dynamic range. In contrast, passive electrostatic headphones rely solely on the listener's audio source without additional power, making them more portable but potentially compromising sound fidelity. Both types appeal to audiophiles, with active models often providing superior performance while passive models offer simplicity and ease of use.</p></p>
