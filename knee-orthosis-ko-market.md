The market is divided into two main types: dynamic orthotics and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics allow for movement while providing support, helping enhance functional mobility and activity. Both types serve specific therapeutic purposes, catering to varied needs in rehabilitation and injury prevention.</p></p>
