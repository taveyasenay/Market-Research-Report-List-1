<p><p>The recycling of lead-acid batteries involves two primary market types: echelon utilization and raw material recycling. Echelon utilization focuses on reusing batteries for secondary applications, extending their lifecycle before they are fully discarded. In contrast, raw material recycling emphasizes reclaiming valuable components, such as lead and sulfuric acid, to be processed and reused in new battery production or other industrial applications. Both approaches contribute to sustainability, reducing environmental impact and promoting resource efficiency in the battery industry.</p></p>
