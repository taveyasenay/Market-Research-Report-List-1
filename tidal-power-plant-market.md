<p><strong>The Tidal Power Plant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-library Unidirectional Station</li><li>Single-library Bi-directional Station</li><li>Double-library Bi-directional Station</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tidal power plants can be categorized into three main market types based on their design and functionality. </p><p>1. **Single-library Unidirectional Station**: Generates electricity during one tidal phase, harnessing energy as water flows in one direction.</p><p>2. **Single-library Bi-directional Station**: Captures energy from both incoming and outgoing tides, optimizing energy production from two tidal phases.</p><p>3. **Double-library Bi-directional Station**: Utilizes dual reservoirs and can generate power from both tidal flows more efficiently, maximizing energy capture across various tidal conditions.</p></p>
