<p><p>The GPS Auto Monitoring System market features two primary types of transmission systems: Wired Transmission Systems and Dial Transmission Mode Systems. </p><p>Wired Transmission Systems use physical cables to relay data, ensuring stable and continuous connectivity, ideal for real-time tracking in fleet management. In contrast, Dial Transmission Mode Systems utilize telephone lines to send periodic updates, offering a simpler, cost-effective solution for tracking vehicles. Each system serves distinct operational needs, balancing reliability and budget considerations in GPS monitoring applications.</p></p>
