<p><p>The rheological additives market is categorized into organic and inorganic types. Organic rheological additives, derived from natural sources or synthesized polymers, enhance viscosity and improve the flow characteristics of products like paints and coatings. In contrast, inorganic rheological additives, typically minerals or metal salts, provide thickening and stabilizing properties in various formulations, including construction materials and personal care products. Both types play crucial roles in controlling texture, consistency, and performance across diverse industries.</p></p>
