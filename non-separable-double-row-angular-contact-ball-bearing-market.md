<p><p>Non-separable double row angular contact ball bearings are categorized into closed and open market types based on their sealing methods. Closed type bearings feature seals or shields that protect against contaminants and retain lubrication, making them ideal for demanding environments. Open type bearings lack these seals, allowing for easier maintenance and lubrication access, suitable for applications where cleanliness is less critical. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the operational requirements and environmental conditions they face.</p></p>
