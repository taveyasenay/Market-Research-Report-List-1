<p><p>The Portable Power Sprayers market primarily features two types: Direct Coupling Type and Belt Driven Type. Direct Coupling Types connect the engine directly to the pump, offering compact designs and efficient power transfer, ideal for small-scale applications. In contrast, Belt Driven Types utilize a system of pulleys and belts, allowing for greater flexibility in pump speed and torque, making them suitable for larger operations. These distinctions cater to diverse consumer needs in agricultural, pest control, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
