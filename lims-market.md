<p><ul><li>On-premise LIMS</li><li>Cloud-based LIMS</li><li>Remotely Hosted LIMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LIMS (Laboratory Information Management Systems) are categorized into three main market types: On-premise LIMS, Cloud-based LIMS, and Remotely Hosted LIMS. On-premise LIMS are installed and operated within a laboratory's infrastructure, offering full control but requiring significant IT resources. Cloud-based LIMS are hosted on the vendor's servers, providing scalable access and lower maintenance costs, ideal for remote work. Remotely Hosted LIMS combine aspects of both, offering the reliability of on-premise systems with the flexibility of cloud access, all managed off-site.</p></p>
