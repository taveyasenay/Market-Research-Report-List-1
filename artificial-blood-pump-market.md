<p><p>The artificial blood pump market includes various types, primarily volumetric and vane type pumps. Volumetric pumps operate by displacing a specific volume of fluid with each cycle, ensuring a consistent flow rate, making them ideal for precise applications. Vane type pumps, on the other hand, utilize rotating vanes to create a continuous flow of blood, providing a more efficient and adaptable solution. Both types cater to different medical needs, enhancing patient outcomes in cardiovascular treatments and artificial circulation.</p></p>
