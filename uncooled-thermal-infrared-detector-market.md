<p><strong>The Uncooled Thermal Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microbolometer IR Detector</li><li>Thermopile IR Detector</li><li>Pyroelectric IR Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled thermal infrared detector market consists of three primary types: Microbolometer IR detectors, Thermopile IR detectors, and Pyroelectric IR detectors. Microbolometers use a change in resistance to detect infrared radiation, making them ideal for imaging applications. Thermopile IR detectors use multiple thermocouples to generate a voltage in response to temperature changes, suitable for non-contact temperature measurement. Pyroelectric IR detectors rely on the pyroelectric effect, providing high sensitivity for motion detection and presence sensing in various applications.</p></p>
