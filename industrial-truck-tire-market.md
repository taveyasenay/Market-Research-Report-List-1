<p><p>The industrial truck tire market features three primary types: pneumatic forklift tires, solid forklift tires, and polyurethane forklift tires. Pneumatic tires, filled with air, offer a cushioned ride suitable for indoor and outdoor applications. Solid tires are made of solid rubber, providing high durability and reduced risk of flats, ideal for heavy-duty environments. Polyurethane tires combine lightweight material with excellent wear resistance, suitable for specific applications requiring precision and minimal noise. Each type caters to different operational needs and environments.</p></p>
