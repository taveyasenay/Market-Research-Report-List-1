<p><p>The pallet lorry market encompasses two main types: manual pallet trucks and electric pallet trucks. Manual pallet trucks are manually operated devices designed for lifting and moving pallets with minimal effort, ideal for light-duty tasks. In contrast, electric pallet trucks use battery power for transportation and lifting, enhancing efficiency and reducing physical strain. The electric variant is preferable for heavier loads and longer distances, making it suitable for warehouses and distribution centers, while manual trucks are favored for their simplicity and lower cost.</p></p>
