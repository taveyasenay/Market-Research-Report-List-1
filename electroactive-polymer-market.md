<p><p>The electroactive polymer market comprises several key types: Conductive Plastics, which are regular polymers infused with conductive materials to impart electrical properties; Inherently Conductive Polymers (ICPs), which are specialized polymers with inherent electrical conductivity; and Inherently Dissipative Polymers (IDPs), designed to control static electricity by dissipating charges rather than conducting them. These categories cater to various applications, including electronics, automotive, and biomedical sectors, driven by the need for lightweight, flexible, and efficient materials with advanced electrical properties.</p></p>
