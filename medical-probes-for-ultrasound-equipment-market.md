<p><p>The medical probes for ultrasound equipment market includes various types, primarily ceramic probes and single crystal probes. Ceramic probes utilize piezoelectric ceramics to produce and receive ultrasound waves, offering durability and effective imaging. Single crystal probes, on the other hand, use a single piezoelectric crystal, providing superior sensitivity and resolution. This advancement allows for enhanced image clarity and deeper tissue penetration. Both types cater to different clinical needs, impacting diagnostic accuracy and patient outcomes in medical imaging.</p></p>
