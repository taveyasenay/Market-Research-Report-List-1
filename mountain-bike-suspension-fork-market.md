<p><ul><li>Air Spring Suspension Fork</li><li>Coil Spring Suspension Fork</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mountain bike suspension forks primarily come in two types: air spring and coil spring. Air spring suspension forks use compressed air to provide adjustable stiffness and weight savings, making them ideal for varied terrains. They offer a smoother ride and can be tuned for different rider preferences. In contrast, coil spring suspension forks utilize metal coils for consistent performance and durability, often preferred for their reliability and simple maintenance. Both types cater to different riding styles and conditions, influencing bike handling and comfort.</p></p>
