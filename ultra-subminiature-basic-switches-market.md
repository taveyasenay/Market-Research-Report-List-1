Open type switches are designed for general applications where environmental exposure is minimal, allowing for ease of installation and cost efficiency. In contrast, sealed type switches provide enhanced protection against dust, moisture, and other contaminants, making them suitable for harsh environments. Both types cater to specific industry needs, with open types typically used in consumer electronics and sealed types found in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
