<p><strong>The Mobile Sterilization Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Loop</li><li>Open Loop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile sterilization solutions are categorized into two main types: closed loop and open loop systems. Closed loop systems are designed to contain the sterilization process within a sealed environment, ensuring maximum control over sterilants and minimizing contamination risks. In contrast, open loop systems operate without such containment, allowing for more flexibility in application but potentially exposing external environments to sterilants. Both types serve diverse needs in healthcare and industrial settings, optimizing sterilization processes based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
