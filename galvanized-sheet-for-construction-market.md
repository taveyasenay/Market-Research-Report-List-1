<p><strong>The Galvanized Sheet for Construction Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro Galvanized Sheet</li><li>Hot Dip Galvanized Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Galvanized sheets are steel plates coated with a protective layer of zinc to enhance corrosion resistance in construction. The Electro Galvanized Sheet is produced through an electroplating process, offering a thinner zinc layer, ideal for applications requiring a smooth finish and moderate corrosion protection. Conversely, Hot Dip Galvanized Sheets are immersed in molten zinc, resulting in a thicker, more robust coating that provides superior durability and long-lasting protection, making it suitable for heavy-duty structural applications.</p></p>
