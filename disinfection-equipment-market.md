<p><p>The disinfection equipment market is divided into two main types: physical methods and chemical methods. Physical methods include techniques like heat treatment, ultraviolet (UV) radiation, and filtration, which eliminate or deactivate pathogens without the use of chemicals. Chemical methods involve the application of disinfectants, such as chlorine, alcohol, or hydrogen peroxide, effective in killing a wide range of microorganisms. Both approaches are essential in ensuring hygiene and safety across various industries, including healthcare, food processing, and water treatment.</p></p>
