<p><strong>The Disposable Resuscitator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-inflating Resuscitator</li><li>Flow-inflating Resuscitator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Disposable Resuscitator market features two primary types: self-inflating and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators automatically refill with air after each compression, making them easy to use in emergencies without a need for additional equipment. In contrast, flow-inflating resuscitators rely on a continuous gas source, allowing for more controlled ventilation and higher pressures, often used in clinical settings. Both types are essential for providing effective respiratory support in various medical scenarios, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
