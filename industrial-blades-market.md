<p><p>The industrial blades market encompasses various manufacturing techniques, including sintering, high-frequency welding, and laser welding. Sintering involves compacting powder materials under heat to create durable blades, ideal for cutting applications. High-frequency welding utilizes electric currents to join metal parts seamlessly, enhancing strength and performance. Laser welding employs focused laser beams to produce precise and clean welds, suitable for intricate designs. These methods cater to diverse industries, ensuring efficiency and reliability in blade production.</p></p>
