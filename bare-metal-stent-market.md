<p><p>A bare metal stent (BMS) is a small, mesh-like device used in angioplasty to open narrowed or blocked arteries. Unlike drug-eluting stents, bare metal stents do not have a medication coating, allowing for immediate endothelial healing but requiring careful management to prevent restenosis. The bare metal stent market is witnessing considerable growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in stent technology, and a rising aging population. </p><p>Innovations in material science and manufacturing techniques have improved the safety and efficacy of BMS, making them a preferred choice in certain clinical scenarios. Additionally, the growing number of percutaneous coronary interventions contributes to market expansion. </p><p>The Bare Metal Stent Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period. Trends such as the development of biocompatible materials and the integration of biosensors in stent design are likely to enhance patient outcomes and drive further adoption. Moreover, increasing awareness about heart health and the expanding healthcare infrastructure in emerging economies are expected to provide additional momentum to the market. Overall, these factors indicate a positive future for the bare metal stent market, aligning with the broader trends in cardiac care and intervention.</p></p>
