<p><p>The dental water jet market encompasses two primary types: countertop oral irrigators and cordless oral irrigators. Countertop oral irrigators are larger units that require a power source and provide a continuous flow of pulsating water, ideal for thorough cleaning and often featuring multiple pressure settings. In contrast, cordless oral irrigators are battery-operated, portable devices that offer convenience and mobility, making them suitable for travelers or those with limited bathroom space. Both types effectively enhance oral hygiene by removing plaque and food particles.</p></p>
