<p><p>The automotive OE lighting market is segmented into three main types: halogen, HID xenon, and LED lights. Halogen lights are traditional incandescent bulbs known for their affordability and ease of replacement, though they offer lower efficiency. HID xenon lights provide brighter illumination and longer lifespan, leveraging gas discharge technology for enhanced visibility. LED lights are the most advanced option, offering superior energy efficiency, durability, and versatility in designs, making them increasingly popular for modern vehicles.</p></p>
