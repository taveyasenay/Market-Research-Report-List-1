<p><p>Carbon fiber in the automotive market is categorized into several types. Sheet Molding Compound (SMC) offers high-strength parts via compression molding. Short Fiber Thermoplastic (SFT) provides flexibility and processability for complex shapes. Long Fiber Thermoplastic (LFT) enhances mechanical properties and impact resistance for structural applications. Prepreg, pre-impregnated with resin, allows for precision in manufacturing lightweight, high-performance components. Other categories may include various hybrid materials and innovative composites, expanding the versatility of carbon fiber in automotive applications.</p></p>
