<p><p>The Copper Clad Aluminum Composite Sheet market is segmented into three main types: inlay, overlay, and edgelay. Inlay involves embedding copper within aluminum, enhancing conductivity while retaining lightweight properties. Overlay features a layer of copper bonded to the aluminum surface, providing improved corrosion resistance and electrical performance. Edgelay refers to copper cladding applied to the edges of aluminum sheets, optimizing strength and conductivity in specific applications. Each type caters to distinct industrial needs, influencing material choice and performance.</p></p>
