<p><p>The IC-Substrate market encompasses various package types including FC-CSP (Flip Chip Chip Scale Package), FC-BGA (Flip Chip Ball Grid Array), CSP (Chip Scale Package), and BGA (Ball Grid Array). FC-CSP features a compact design suitable for high-density applications, while FC-BGA offers enhanced thermal management for powerful chips. CSP is known for its small footprint and efficiency, whereas BGA provides reliable connectivity and is commonly used in larger devices. Each type caters to specific performance, size, and application requirements in electronics.</p></p>
