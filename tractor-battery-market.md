<p><p>The tractor battery market comprises three main types: lithium batteries, lead-acid batteries, and other batteries. Lithium batteries are known for their long lifespan, high energy density, and lightweight, making them increasingly popular for modern tractors. Lead-acid batteries are the traditional choice, valued for their affordability and reliability, despite being heavier and having a shorter lifespan. Other battery types, which may include gel or AGM batteries, offer specific advantages in terms of maintenance and performance in various agricultural applications.</p></p>
