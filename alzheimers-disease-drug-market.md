Memantine, an NMDA receptor antagonist, protects neurons by modulating glutamate activity, addressing moderate to severe stages. Rivastigmine, another acetylcholinesterase inhibitor, is available as both a capsule and transdermal patch, offering flexible administration. Together, these drugs form a critical segment of Alzheimer's treatment, aiming to manage symptoms and improve quality of life for patients.</p></p>
